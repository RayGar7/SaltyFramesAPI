Inputs

White arrows indicate which direction to press.

Black arrows indicate which direction to press twice.

Horizontal Attack.

Vertical Attack.

Kick Attack Move.

Guard Move.

Black letters will indicate which attack button to hold down.

When a button is shown small it should be pressed briefly before moving on to the next input.

When a small button is right next to a bug button it means the button is supposed to be pressed immediately.

A small button right next to a big button and small button next to a big button with some space in between are equivalent.

The asterisk stands for OR.

The underscore is used interchangeably with an asterisk.

The plus will indicate when you have to press multiple inputs together.
